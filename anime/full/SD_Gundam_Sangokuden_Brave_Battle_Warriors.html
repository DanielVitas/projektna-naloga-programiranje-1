This is my first review in My Anime, so I am a little bit excited.
Overall, this one was a great series. Maybe it could have been a little better at the ending, maybe not. However it had lovely and beautiful art, absolutely amazing characters - especially Lu Bu, the feared general warrior - and a fascinating story, which I understand is based on a classic Chinese novel. I know that the concept of chibi gundams acting as people might be weird for many, but I found it similar to Bionicles and more compelling.
My final rating is 9.5 out of 10.
