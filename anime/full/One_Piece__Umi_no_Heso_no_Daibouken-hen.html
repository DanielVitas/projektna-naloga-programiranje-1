Synopsis

The Straw Hats encounter a city in the middle of a whirlpool, called the Ocean's Navel, that is being destroyed by giant monsters. These monsters supposedly protect a treasure capable of granting wishes, but in the process of defeating these guardians, the crew ends up releasing another evil. (aired after Episode 53)

This is by far the most enjoyable, entertaining and well-writen of all the specials and ova’s. I frequent it above all others since it will immidiately lift your spirits everytime your feeling down.

The story, characters and the setting of the plot, all fit extremely well with the big upcomming adventure mood that the first two season have. You can really tell that the makers of this ova, did their uttermost best to emulate the entire scope and feel of the story and the characters.

Most of the movies can’t hold a candle to it. If one should make a list of all the movies, specials, ova’s, extra’s and featurettes in order of greatness and epicness this ova will certainly get fifth maybe even third place.

It’s a must watch for anyone who considers him or herself an one piece fan, furthermore this special can also be seen by somebody completely new to one piece, I personally think this special is a good way to get people to start watching one piece.
