To begin with, I have to say this is better than the original anime. But then again, I'm not sure why I even bothered watching it.

To make it short, I decided to watch this because I'm a little OCD about leaving things unfinished. If I start an anime, I must finish every season and OVA and special and movie-- I just HAVE to. I didn't like Amnesia as a series, but I ended up here anyhow-- but I liked this a lot more than the original show.

The story was simple and fast-paced enough that I didn't get too bored. It had a simple point, got resolved quickly, and made some sense.

The art, like usual, was freaking gorgeous. The weird costumes and stuff irked me like usual, but the eyes and backgrounds were phenomenal.

The opening and closing themes were astounding like usual, though the background music sometimes seemed displaced.

The characters were eh. They were even more out of character than they were in the original anime, but they were simple enough.

I enjoyed this enough to get through the episode, but I probably won't ever watch it again.

I would recommend this if you: liked the original anime, like cute sorta-crack episodes, like reverse harems

I would NOT recommend this if you: despised Amnesia, like plot, despise reverse harems

Have a great day everyone!
