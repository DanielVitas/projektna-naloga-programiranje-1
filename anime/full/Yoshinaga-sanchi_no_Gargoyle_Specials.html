Wow, I knew it was going to be short, but this short?

There are a few episodes, it can take 5 minutes, or just 1&frac12; minute to finish one episode of this. To bad it's short cause it's actually funny.

One of our lead characters falls asleep on a magic book, which makes her think she's Alice (a parody in this case) in Wonderland, and all the other known characters will be the bad or good guys, she goes through al kinds of fairy tails and stories.

Th...e story is very funny, yet simple, while the art is a bit better then in the series it's still pretty basic, same goes for the sound.

The way some characters are being potraited in these specials is very funny, most of they're good/bad behaviours get sprung out like hell, and good too.

Really enjoyable once you've seen the original series.
