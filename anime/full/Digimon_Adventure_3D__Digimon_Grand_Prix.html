Background

Digimon Adventure 3D: Digimon Grand Prix! was screened at Sanrio Puroland's Yume no Time Machine attraction between July 20, 2000, and June 23, 2002. It was later screened at Tobidasu! 3D Toei Anime Matsuri on October 3, 2009.
