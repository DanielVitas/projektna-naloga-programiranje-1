Synopsis

Continuing where the first season left off, the sudden increase of Thanatos with the disappearance of the Aube Hunters has now left Arcadia in ruin. Desperation reigns high as the Religious Organization fights to battle the Thanatos. Six months had already past since Hidamari mansion burned to ashes, the Aube Hunters, once hailed as heroes, is now blamed for the appearance of Thanatos. Nevertheless, hope still exists for the reappearance of the Queen's Egg one day, who will become the savior of Arcadia. Unknown to many however, the Aube Hunters have actually been silently waiting for the awakening of Angelique, who has been asleep the entire time.

Neo Angelique Abyss was one of the first series of animes that I've watched. Watching the first season made me want to continue onto watching the second season.

The story and plot was well written. As I finished the first season, I continued on watching the second season. I couldn't stop watching the anime. I spent the whole day watching the second season. The anime just kept me wanting to have more. By the time I realized that the second season had ended, it had me craving for more. It is unfortunate that there isn't more.

The art in this anime was done well. The details of each character were magnificent. Overall, I found this anime enjoyable. This certainly was not an anime that I would put off to watch later.
