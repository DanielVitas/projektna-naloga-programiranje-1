This is a short series of ecchi fluff nonsense and fanservice, so if you don't like that then I wouldn't recommend this.

On the other hand, if you do like that... well boy howdy do I have the thing for you. I wasn't expecting to enjoy this, especially with the many negative reviews. But it was surprisingly entertaining for the most part, though got a bit too rediculous even for me a couple of times. The episodes are pure storyless silliness, which normally I would criticize. The main reason I am not panning this is because the episodes were all 3 minutes long, keeping them from dragging on. This entire series of shorts will only take you as long as watching one episode of a normal anime would.

For the majority of the episodes, the animation was more towards a storybook format, with mostly still frames panning across the screen to vaguely give you an idea of what is happening (or provide fanservice). The actual art looked fine, though was mixed in with real photos occasionally which was mildly odd.

The sound was as silly as the shorts, but it worked. You are essentially listening to the story, and the sound effects and music connect to the dialogue ably.

I rated this a 7 because for what it is, I think it fulfills it's purpose well. If you want some brainless fanservice absurdity starring the NGNL cast then this is for you. If not, steer clear.
