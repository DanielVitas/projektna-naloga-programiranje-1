Edit
Synopsis

A series of shorts broadcasted on Kids Station. The star a Panda (with other animals present) as they do quick skits usually revolving around singing, dancing, and exercise.

Edit
Background

The characters are also frequently used in commercials for various companies. The series is most notable for being the official mascot of Okayama Toyopet, a Japanese Toyota car dealership.
