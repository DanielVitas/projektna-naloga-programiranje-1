Yawara helps out some kids who are being bullied by teaching them judo. The results are predictable. It's completely disconnected from the rest of the Yawara series. It's more of an anti-bullying PSA than entertainment.

There's no reason to watch this other than completeness. Watch the Yawara series or the Olympics OVA instead.
