I rarely enjoy "special" shorts, but these were pretty charming - the first four, anyway. (The last two weren't bad, but were a definite drop-off in quality; otherwise, I'd have scored an 8.) It's six shorts of the characters being silly and playing off each other, just as they did in the comedic scenes from the show. Plus, for those of us in it for the queer content, these go a long way toward repairing the overwhelming no-homo of the second half of season 2. We get Natsuki trying to kiss Riko, Midori obsessing over Reina's chest (because they look like fermata!), and Reina straight-up leaving her class's school festival preparations because she wants to see Kumiko in a maid outfit. The one thing I didn't like was the relative dearth of Asuka, Nozomi, and Mizore. Not groundbreaking, but definitely among the better anime shorts I've seen.

Story and Character: 6-7/10
Hibike Euphonium 2 Specials is a good relief from the plot heavy drama of the main series. The comedic services provided through each individual segment of episodes really displayed another side of the characters that we would not have seen, supplementing the world with personalities and life. However the comedy is decent, and only gave me a few smirks but nonetheless not exactly the best

Art: 5/10
The art certainly can be seen as a flaw, especially since this was made by the great Kyoto Animations, however considering this is a 2 minute - 7 episode special, it can perhaps be overlooked

Sound: 5/10
Really didn't stand out to me, the voice acting is the same except more suited towards skits and gags for each character, and the background music is very fitting to the comedic setting, really giving off the vibe that its main purpose is to focus on specific characters, but other than that not much more to it

Enjoyment 6/10
Watched it because I love the series, came in without expecting anything, and came out feeling the exact same

Overall
I recommend this to people who have watched and enjoyed the series due to the fact that this really wouldn't stand out as much as the main stories, it's main purpose is to give a few giggles and explore the world of Hibike Euphonium
