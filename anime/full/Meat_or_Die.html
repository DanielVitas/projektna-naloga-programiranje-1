Often I'll find a series and dislike it, but this series goes beyond that. I disliked this series so much it actually annoyed me. Short anime are often not very good, and this is the most extreme example.

It's a crazy, brainded show consisting of three unfunny jokes repeated over and over. They constantly reference famous video games and movies to try and get laughs from viewers, with the lazy punchline just being "look its that thing you know". I understand that sense of humour changes from person to person, but I can't see anyone enjoying any of the jokes. The way they're presented is just lazy.

I cannot think of anything positive to say about this series other than "at least it ended".
