Tesagure! Bukatsumono is unlikely anime, most of their talk and activity is talking about school clubs and come up with "what if" and new things to add that might be good but still related with with the club's activity that being talked about.
But that is not the charm of this anime... at all, it was just the foreplay.
The charm point of this anime is the"ad lib" corner, yes ad lib (no script).
At certain time the characters or i should type... the VA(voice actress(es)) do a conversation with no script, you could hear their laugh their face off, make a stupid joke, ridiculous puns, yawn, stomach grumble, awkward silent, and real angry person voice.
there's big downside in this so called "encore" or "second season", at some episode they use footage from 1st season and cut their movement motion.
Well, the reason behind was said because the animator can't make it in time and also budget problem... and lazy. This is said by the characters in the anime which i doubt it since this is scripted.


Story : what story? again...

Art : don't start, 2.5D and 3D CG. Like Kemono Friends.

Sound : sometimes good, sometimes bad. You get the gits.

Character : Interesting in their own ways.

Enjoyment : Let me tell you this,
at every one or two months i watched over again this series... for the ad lib corner of course.

Conclusion is your life as veteran anime watcher not complete if you have not watch this.
