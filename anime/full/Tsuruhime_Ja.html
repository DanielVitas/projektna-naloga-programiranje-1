This is another one of the many fine works produced by Madhouse. As a fan of madhouse, I found it to be absolutely perfect. While it does have many flaws I was able to overlook them because it was made by Madhouse. If it was made by any other company I would have hated it.

In conclusion, this series is a masterpiece and I highly recommend it. According to my parents I watched this show when I was a toddler and I don't actually remember anything about it, in fact I think they are mixing it up with Spongebob.
