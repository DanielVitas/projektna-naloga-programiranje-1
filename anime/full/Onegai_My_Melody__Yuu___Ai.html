Synopsis

Set before the events of Sweets Dance Princess, it focuses about My Melody's summer camping in Mary Land and how her friendship with Kuromi is becoming better, despite the upsetting experiences and misunderstandings she had during camp.

(Source: ANN)

Double feature with the first Jewelpet film, Jewelpet Movie: Sweets Dance Princess.
