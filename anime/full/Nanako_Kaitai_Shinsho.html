An amazing piece of film that is there to break the mold of what film very much is, Amazing Nurse Nanako (or Nanako Kaitai Shinsho as it is also called) does away with all constraints of what limits a normal production.

It goes about this by completely doing away with any semblance of a "Plot". Now this maybe awful how can a show not have a plot and still be well made you say, well the absence of any sort of coherent plot is there to show us that our lives are as nearly as unstructured as a story with no plot and is there to serve as commentary on why we need to stop making our art so structured and simple to reflect our chaotic lives and lifestyles.

Now the art of this show is hideous to the untrained eye and makes you want to gouge your eyes out if you are a simpleton not understanding the underlying themes of Amazing Nurse Nanako, but you have to understand why the art looks like you just threw fecal matter into a blender. The art of Nanako forces you to see everything in a new light, it shows you that indeed everything around you is gross and horrifying any you shouldn't be going to the world of animation to fix your issues or to escape the disgusting world around you.

The sound serves a similar purpose to the visuals and art of Nanako, by making you want to get a rapid fire shotgun repeatedly fired next to your ear so your eardrums melt away to the point that your not deaf but rather hearing negative sound. This capitalizes on the point that not everything you hear in life will be pleasant and most of it will be painful screeching.

The Characters in Amazing Nurse Nanako are truly outstanding by making them all 2 dimensional, flat, and not giving them any sort of development Nanko displays that in heart we as humans never change and will continue to follow our base instincts.

In closing Amazing Nurse Nanako talks about the very fundamentals on what it is to be human with a revolutionizing story, art, and sound direction that can only be Nanako for anyone considering watching this anime i highly recommend using your time to instead find any sort of fire arm or rope and just end it all because this anime proves why were are completely doomed as a species.

10/10 would watch again.
