Synopsis

First-grader Masaya is a crybaby despite his large stature, so he can't walk by himself on the dark path to school. The one who holds his hand is Akiyo, a tiny but capable second-grader.

Masaya wants to be strong like Akiyo and admires her, but one day, something happens that leaves Akiyo in tears.

To lift Akiyo's spirits, Masaya goes trekking to the distant Ipponsugi forest to find the spotted bellflowers that Akiyo loves.

(Source: ANN)

Okay so no review have been written about this short piece- so I figured I might as well write a brief one. This anime is a short 25 minute film about one 1st grader and one 2nd grader. This is a very lighthearted story that definitely isn't going to be objectively a "great" or standout anime- but its cute, fun, and a nice feel-good story. It follows the 1st grader (which is a boy) as he is determined to travel all alone to a distant place in order to make the 2nd grader happy through getting her one of her favorite flowers.


There's really not much else to say about the story. Characters aren't developed that much but what can you expect from such a short film right? And it doesn't even matter. This isn't the type of anime you watch for the story or character development. Have a bad day? Just watch this anime. I found myself smiling through parts of it and it tells a nice, cute short story.


Would I recommend this? For sure. It isn't for everyone- but its a simple anime that is sure to either brighten your day up or help you take a break from the more serious anime's you might be watching.
