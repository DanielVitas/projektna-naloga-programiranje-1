Persona 3 the Movie Meets "Walkman" is the alternate ending I hoped to see from the Persona 3 films. It stands as an absolute masterpiece for the Persona 3 storyline... scratch that, it stands as a masterpiece for the Persona series in general. You never know where the plot is going to go, from the beginning to the end of this feature presentation. You instantly feel engrossed into the Walkman alternate storyline that ATLUS had wrote for this to take. Makoto's headphones taking their upgrade into Sony headphones also gives this a feel that this is something new.

I believe this is an unspoken masterpiece. From the time when the first step to the music starting really gets me going. Persona has never been taken to these heights.
