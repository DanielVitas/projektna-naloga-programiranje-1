Essentially two short and completely different episodes in one, the first continues where the series left off and shows what did (and didn't) happen in the "excite" room, whilst the second has the poor old protagonist suffering under the girls misinterpretations of "ball" games

The first part is certainly better than the second, both with the story and the animation, with the latter being a bit too tortuous and seemingly losing a bit of quality in the animation department.

Well worth seeing, if only for the concluding part of the main series

Sound and music is just fine, with the usual title and end credit music.
