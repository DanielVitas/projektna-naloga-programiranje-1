Synopsis

DVD-only special of REC. This is a special episode 8 of 10 on the DVD, which is episode 7.5 of the original 9. It is entitled "The Unforgiven".

Fumihiko and Aka go out to eat and come across Tanaka, who had just been dumped by her boyfriend. She pressures him to drink with her for the night and Aka becomes jealous. When Tanaka is drunk, Fumihiko takes her home, much to Aka's dismay and suspicion. The next day, Tanaka tells Aka that nothing happened.

(Source: Wikipedia)

Rec: Yurusarezarumono is an ova that comes after episode 7, adding more to the story and the characters in it.

Story 9/10 :
"Fumihiko and Aka go out to eat and come across Tanaka, who had just been dumped by her boyfriend. She pressures him to drink with her for the night and Aka becomes jealous." -quote from the ova's page. Its a funny short episode and it adds up to the whole anime.

Art 8/10:
Same as the main series, looks very good for its time.

Sound 8/10:
it doesnt add any new soundtrack, tho for the anime itself i gave 8 for the sound because its very good and the opening is great~

Character 10/10:
In the ova we get to see alot of character comming out from Tanaka and Aka, really suprised me that an ova added this much!

Enjoyment 10/10:
I enjoyed this ova alot just as I enjoyed the whole series^^

Overall 9/10:
when I watched this ova, It appeard to be episode 8 out of 10. It was so on point that it took me 1 hour to realise that what I've been watching was an OVA and not a normal episode. the ova is great, feels just like the main series and it is really funny!

-I really recommend watching this episode right after eipsode 7.
