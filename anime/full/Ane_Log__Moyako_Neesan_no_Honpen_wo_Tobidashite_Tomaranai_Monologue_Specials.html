First there was the rather good (and very short) series. And then there was this... Unfortunately...

First off, it doesn't really have anything to do with the series - there is a history less, gym session and a talk-in style episode. Only the latter two really continue with Ane's obsession with her brother, and then only slightly, and amongst much filler.

Aside from that, there is also the animation problem - basically Flash based animation, done on the cheap. Whilst the characters certainly look like they do in the main series, there is also a lack of definition and of course restricted movement.

Only the second episode is of any real interest, as thats the only time Ane gets really worked up. Whilst it does also happen in the third one, it's not so fun.

All in all, not worth watching
