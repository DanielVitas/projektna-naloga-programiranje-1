Synopsis

After the events of Invasion, Johnny Rico has been demoted to the rank of colonel and relocated to a Martian satellite to train a new batch of troopers. Unfortunately, these troopers are some of the worst low-performing Rico has ever trained as they're Martians and don't take the war seriously. Mars overall has low support for the war as they see their planet unaffected by the bug conflict and even suggested pulling out from the war. Because of their laid back attitude, the denizens of Mars wasn't ready when the bugs attacked. Unknown to everyone, Sky Marshall Amy Snapp executes her plans for power.

(Source: Wikipedia)

This movie is a continuation of Invasion, if you haven't seen that movie I highly suggest you watch that first.


CONTAINS MINOR STORY-SPOILERS:

Story:

It starts briefly after the prequel called Invasion. Rico has been demoted to Colonel and is in charge of training a bunch of Martians with no combat-experience called the 'Lost Patrol'. The lost patrol has some interesting, humoristic characters that don't really seem to understand how terrifying the war actually is. (...)

Sky Marshal Snapp is the main villain this time around, capturing and holding C.Jenkins against his will(...) ultimately to destroy the planet Mars.(...) Since she can't simply destroy (by means of a Q-bomb) the peaceful world of Mars she hates so much (because the martians are against the war) she has 'planted' a colony of bugs on Mars. This is where the Lost Patrol comes in. (...) They have to stop the Q-bomb from going of and save their planet.

I personally don't like the story/plot so much, however, I'm a forgivable person.

Would you like to know more?

Characters:

There's really not that much to say, all the characters from the Lost Patrol are 'fresh' troopers with no actual combat experience.
Each one of them has some screen time (some more than others...) and they really did put a humoristic touch on them which I didn't mind so much. Some of them show some character growth, but it's a movie, so I think they did well with the time they had.

Would you like to know more?

Animation:

Just like the prequel Invasion it looks really great, it's not perfect, but it's pretty spot on. No complaints here.

Would you like to know more?

Overall:

I did enjoy this movie, not so much as the Original or Invasion, but I really liked the humoristic touch they put on this one. It feels fresh, eventho I generally like the 'serious' approach regarding movies, this wasnt bad at all.
Keep in mind it's still Starship Troopers, if you do that, you'll absolutely love it regardless of it's flaws.

Would you like to know more..?
