Synopsis

Shu is from Datania, a country that makes young children work. He and his friends were trying to escape when they met the Returners. The Returners are some kind of rebels to Datania. Shu goes with the Returners to fight Zero, Nines, and his men who are after DT, a special substance inside humans.

I'm a huge fan of the Mecha genre, it is my second most favorite genre, but have trouble finding Mecha that hit high on my favorites, because when it comes to Mecha, I end up nitpicking every flaw I can find while not realizing it...
Seeing no reviews on the anime, I thought I might give it a watch, and write a review, if I could finish it, and I did, now, I'm sorry if my review is bad, this is my first proper review, I had reviews made before, but they were poorly structured, and not well written, so I deleted them all, and researched for a while on how I could do better, I won't be deleting this review though!
If this review of mine gets nicely supported, I will consider writing more reviews later...
Story = 6, I don't have much to say with the story, I think it would have been fine to show awesome fights, but to say the least, there was actually little to no fights, and the fights could be summed up as mediocre or poor.
Art = 7, I don't generally go super analytical on the art and animation, I didn't see much in the way of bad animation, nothing looked great, and I do prefer 90s art-style to that of majority of other art-styles, so 7 is where I think it should stand
Sound = 4, and this was the part I hated the most about the anime, even the voice acting sounded bad, in which I generally can't tell if Japanese VA are good or bad, this is the first time I've found Japanese VA I can't stand, and music wasn't so great either, which also don't often find many songs I actually can't stand, it got to the point, I just muted the sound but kept watching!
Characters = 4, Okay so I do think that is quite a low score, and to be honest, my reason is because 75% of the time I was watching this, I thought I was watching a childish version of Evangelion, now I don't generally hate an anime for being generic, I can look over that flaw majority of times, but it was just too hard not to for this anime when most of the characters and even the art style looked exactly the same to Evangelion, and that's coming from someone who liked Shinji in the first episode! But hey even if the characters didn't feel like copy and paste, the plot didn't feel so great either!
My overall rating is a 5/10, I almost put it at a 6, but at episode 21, I had the feeling the ending would be absolute utter crap, and what do you know it was, it was like F- you read the manga... I could say how, it's not too much of a spoiler, but I will re frame, as I don't want my review to be taken down by someone who has a different opinion on what could be classed as a spoiler. Oh yeah and also good luck finding the manga, I searched 6 different sites, before I stopped caring, the fact that only 1 person has rated the manga, really does show how hard it is to find the manga!
Oh though if you think you may enjoy it more, then I think it might still be worth giving a try, despite the generic characters, bad sound and bullsh- ending, thank you for reading!
