Synopsis

A Christmas commercial campaign funded by AFLAC.

2 episodes show the ducks acting like Santa with a song sung by songstress Mayuminu. A 3rd commercial was made combining the previous 2 with extra footage and a song sung by Fukushima Prefectural Asaka Reimei school choir.
