Synopsis

After interfering with a top secret mission, the Punisher is taken into custody by S.H.I.E.L.D. Agent and Avenger, Black Widow. At the orders of Director Nick Fury, Punisher and Black Widow are sent on a mission to stop Leviathan, a global terrorist organization that plans to sell stolen S.H.I.E.L.D. technology to the highest bidder. Now, the vigilante and spy must work together to prevent this technology from falling into the wrong hands. The fate of the world, and of the Avengers, hangs in the balance.

The Japan Blu-ray/DVD release date is September 3rd, 2014.

I'm not overly familiar with Punisher's character, but I would say he brought a lot of darkness into this film: the action had more violence to it than usual, although it was still kept mostly off-screen for the sake of this title being aimed at a younger generation.

While the story was entertaining, I wasn't overly fond of Black Widow's portrayal in this one. Her being one of the titular characters, that was a real downer for me, personally - not to speak of the somewhat unconventional, unnecessary romantic element that should have worked, but felt a little flat and one-dimensional despite the life they tried to breathe into it.

An appearance from the other Avengers was a nice touch, for sure.

Story/character
-Has a good amount of depth. Will keep you guessing. Even in the ending you will be surprised.
- story stayed true in regard to each character persona
- An ideal paring, the punisher and the black widow. Both want justice but have different views on approach. Punisher's is fact based while Black widow's shows in emotion. Even more so how they ended up blending smoothy without changing much on who they are. As can be seen in the boss scene.

Art
Good blend not only to the movie but on the marvel characters we know and love.

Sound- no issues

Enjoyment
I know what you may be thinking another action movie right? What makes it different from the rest? Fair question. I suppose part of that answer lies in the message behind it. The message is here is not just one but a couple. In short they all tend to revolve around perspective. No one is saying one is right and one is wrong it is quite the opposite in fact. While any open minded person can tell you there is more than one side to things while that is true that is not the full story. The full story is all sides are good and bad no matter the turn out it is just the town job to determine which ways more in their favor.

For example what punishments should thieves be faced with? death? Money charges? While in our hearts we want them to stop all together we don't know what it is they them selves are faced with. You see while it would be nice to live in a world where everyone has money and is happy the truth is money is limited. Since it is limited that means there has to be some sort of division distribution. We all know there are wealthy people so there has to poor. Going back to the thieves they could trying to pay bills or pay off criminals who stole their love ones. I know i am going off topic with this ramble but i am trying to open your mind to the message of the show on perspective. What you think is not what you think and therefore can't be brought on.
