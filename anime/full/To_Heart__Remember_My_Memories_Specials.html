A short review for a short special, in which various To Heart ~Remember My Memories~ characters fight. That really is all the it is, they try to put some story into it but don't do a very good job.
This is a warning to not download, or even look for it. It's not good, it has 2% relevance to the original story, and...it's not good.

Unlike the series, these fights are more male oriented, as there are many panty shots, tearing clothes (reminded me of Ikkitousen), and pervy shots.

If you liked the original To Heart, you probably won't like this.
If you liked Remember My Memories, you probably won't like this.
If you haven't watched either, I wouldn't reccomend it.

Story:
Pretty lame, they are just randomly fighting for...I don't know. They do do a few humorous parodies though.

Art:
The art/animation is pretty good. I mean, normally it's hard to fail in this section anyways. I honestly prefer the Remember My Memories art style over the original To Heart style.

Sound:
No comment on the sound, it was fine, nothing to make it really good, but nothing for it to be considered bad either.

Characters:
The characters were really out of character, only 2 of the people that fight are actual people who fight do fighting as their character in To Heart.

Enjoyment/Overall:
I didn't really catch the story too well, nor did I enjoy it, but I did enjoy _some_ of the fighting. When I say some like, I mean none of them, cuz all of them were pretty bad.
The ecchi wasn't too bad though, in fact some of it was pretty funny :p
