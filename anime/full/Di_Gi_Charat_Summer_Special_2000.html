Synopsis

The Di Gi Charat Summer Special was a four episode series that aired over two days, August 22, 2000 to August 23, 2000. This was the first series that Piyoko appeared in and the plot involves her attempting to hold Dejiko hostage for ransom.

• Summer Special 2000 Episode 1 - Here comes Pyoko-nyo!
• Summer Special 2000 Episode 2 - It's the Black Gamers-nyo
• Summer Special 2000 Episode 3 - The Return of Abarenbou
• Summer Special 2000 Episode 4 - Summer Bug

(Source: AniDB & Wikipedia)
