Synopsis

Tomoya Mikami's life changed one day when his girlfriend Ayaka was hit by a car and killed on her way to meet Tomoya. He blamed himself for her death and never was the same after the accident. He began acting callously towards Yue and his other friends. Furthermore, every time Tomoya gets involved with a new girl, he sees images of Ayaka in his dreams. He decides he needs to find a way to say goodbye to his lost love once and for all, while dealing with several side romances at the same time.

(Source: ANN)

Ok so straight to the review. This anime really sucks. I am a major romance anime lover and if you have seen any anime like Bokura ga Ita, Itazura na Kiss, Kimi Ga Nozomu Eien, or even OVAs like Ajimu or Iriya no Sora you will hate this. The story was confusing and it was terrible.

Story:
Overall it was bad. Seems like there was no new ideas put into it. They stuffed the drama into this anime like it was nothing. The story went at a pace 10x faster than anything I have seen before. Also the relationships between the main character and the girls is really rushed. VERY CLICHE.

Art:
Art is decent not very good art quality but its worse than probably 80% of the other anime out there released in 2003 and later.

Sound:
Didn't pay attention so I guess that means it was just background music with not much meaning. The music fit the scenes but wasn't anything special

Character:
already stated very rushed. the main characters story is confusing at the beginning and the watcher has to fill in the blanks. Characters are much like any other romance/drama. boy likes girl, girl likes boy. thats all.

Enjoyment:
only thing pulling me through this was the fact it is only 3 episodes. Like i said almost no enjoyment compared to the listed anime above.

Overall:
Could have been MUCH better but it feels like the company just wanted some extra cash or something. not much depth to the characters and the story was terrible.

This series absolutely epitomizes sub par quality.

Reading the synopsis I assumed "hey, this might actually be a decent watch given that it is 3 episodes and I just want something quick to watch out of the corner of my eye while I do other stuff."

Yeah, no. the series was utterly cliche, but in fairness I did kind of expect that.

The art? Looked like something out of a low budget adult animated series.

The sound? Honestly, that was probably the best bit out of the whole thing, I quite enjoyed the music. Though I usually enjoy the music in every series simply because I enjoy music.

Character development. Oh. HERE we go. It's 3 episodes long. Character development? No such thing. The first episode was basically 12 episodes of a similar romance series crammed into one rushed low quality episode with awful art, and what may be the worst voice acting I've heard since the Bible Black dub.

As something to watch because you have no time and just want a quick 3 episodes or something, I would tell you to watch this, but then I would remember you might as well watch 3 episodes of a series that is actually halfway decent and then finish it later.
