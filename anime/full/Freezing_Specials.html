Going into these specials after watching the main story I was thrown rather off guard. These specials do not have a story they appear to be a compilation of ideas that the creators threw in to interest viewers, they interest the viewers by providing much more borderline hentai content in as well as making the specials be full of humour.

I find that the characters did not act entirely the same as how they did in the main story I feel because of this the characters shown felt like different characters all together, despite this the art design and sound was as good as in the actual series and overall they were rather fun to watch at the end of the main series as something to lighten the mood.

I do recommend watching these after the main series as to not give false impressions on the characters and to also entertain yourself.
