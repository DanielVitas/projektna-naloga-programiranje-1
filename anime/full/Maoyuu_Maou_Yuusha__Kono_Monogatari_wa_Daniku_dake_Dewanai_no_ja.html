This anime special recap of Maoyuu Maou Yuusha is from episode 1-9 as told by a "Story Teller" and his young pupil.

It can be linked to similar specials as the Log Horizon and Sword Art Online episode recaps, it simply tries to bring people who have watch episode one to nine, but then took a break from the series.

It does do a fairly good job of doing what I aims to do, it recaps various major parts of the story, but it does manage to miss out of fairly crucial parts, like meeting certain characters, so it'd be very hard to then watch the next episode to complete the series.

The main reason I'd watch this, is simply for the narration over the summary, it brings some new things which makes this recap much better then it would previously be.

This is only a recap episode, it doesn't have to be watched, not pertinent to the story. That being said, I don't think you could watch this as a substitute for episodes 1-9. It leaves out a lot of important information. Some of those concepts are somewhat complicated, and require more than a sentence to fully explain. They leave out some characters in this recap, as well as some plot points. However, the way the did this was interesting. Instead of having a narrator give an overview, they had "storytellers", which was a nice change of pace from usual recap episodes. Brownie points for that.
