Synopsis

The final DVD volume contains two specials:

Tokimeki no Yuuki
Episode 18 (sometimes labeled 17.5), which was never broadcast on TV. It features the swim captain, Utsumi Koayu.

Tokimeki no Sentaku
Episode 25.5, a recap of all episodes but the last, which was initially displayed at the Konami booth for the Tokyo International Anime Fair in 2007.

I was browsing towards some old animes that I enjoyed a lot back than and came across to Tokimeki Memorial ~Only Love~ OVA.

Tokimeki Memorial ~Only Love~ OVA provides nothing major towards the original series. The first episode is some kind of an extra episode between 17-18 introducing one new character along some new scenes. The second one is basicly a recap of the main storyline.

The art, sound and character remained exactly the same. The enjoyment dropped a little and is no where as good as the series. I found it very interesting to watch the recap episode and telling me what the main storyline actually was since I forgotten it.
