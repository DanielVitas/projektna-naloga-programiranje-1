Wanna pretend to play a visual novel? Because that is the experience you gain from watching this.

Two six minute episodes that take place right before the epic concert at the cultural festival. Touma (best girl) and Setsuna board a train together headed towards the school. One of them in each episode is walked in on while changing by Haruki and the two share flustered dialogue together. Why the door wasn't locked? Who knows, but this is anime so we'll let it slide.

I enjoyed hearing the fantastic WA2 OST's again, but there wasn't much value to be had here and this is not essential to the anime itself.
