I'll keep this review short since this is basically the normal series, except a little bit more.

......... *Warning: For any of you who haven't watched the original series these are huge spoilers*

What else do you want me to say, this is basically what the specials is, nothing different really happens. Nozaki is still as clueless as ever, Chiyo is still heads over heels for Nozaki, etc.

It actually pretty much continues from where the original series left off.

To be honest though.... you might not want to watch it. In the end, it's simply a teaser, it's like if you were a dog. And your owner gave you food, see you're satisfied with that. You knew how much you were getting, and you were fine with that. But then the owner starts tempting you by giving you a very small treat. Then you get all excited, anticipating more, and then in the end.... you're left practically disappointed. Plus how it was split up into 6 different parts was kind of unnecessary....

Gekkan Shoujo Nozaki Kun Specials is really just one episode split into 6 parts.
And since you've already seen the series, you know how awesome and funny this will be.
I'll give it to you as brief as possible.

This series of specials is about the beach event commonly found in romcom anime. Yeah, it's hilarious. The drama club takes a trip to the beach and nozaki and his team joins in. Some episodes are for the preparation of the trip and some show different situations at the beach. So, like I said before, this is an episode divided into 6 parts. You've got all your main characters in it and well, that's all it is. BEACH EVENT FTW!!!

Overall, if I see it as an episode of the series, I would rate is as one of the best.
