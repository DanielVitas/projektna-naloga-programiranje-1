Story
Utter confusion is a good sum up for this. Takes place in past when top hats and hoop dresses were common. Going off the complete destruction the characters have on all that surrounds them demonstrates disorder. While their attire suggests compliance and dignity. Perhaps indication for one to cut lose?

Sound
I suggest turning down the volume for it like heavy metal. Playing at unnecessary high level. The song is like a blend between Yolanda Be Cool & DCUP - We No Speak Americano & Scatman John - Scatman. Scatman would cover the bizarre word choices. While We no speak americano sound similar to the tempo of the beat. The idea of a creation between the two is not a bad match since they both rush. Although, i wish the pronunciation wasn't rushed.

art
similar to old style video games that were a common feature on sega. Where the characters move in one direction and the back drop moves in the other. Shortly progresses into attributing old style films where such things like:black and white portrays and loading circles around numbers.
