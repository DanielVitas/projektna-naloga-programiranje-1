I would not call this a Sequel there is about 3 minutes of new footage, the rest is just one of the pilots talking about his experiences threw out the series. It is like a flash back episode, I recommend watching this after you have viewed the series, as the ova is a major spoiler. But I was expecting more since it was called a Sequel, if you are looking for more story content don't bother watching the OVA, if you just want to see more goddess go right ahead.

A little note: Don't go by my rateings for art and sound, I am not the best judge.
