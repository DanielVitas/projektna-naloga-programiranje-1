This is a super short review for a super short video. There is no story. There is no dialogue. There is no 'real' animation, it is more like a slide show. This is a set of panned still frames set to music with a little bit of text at the beginning and end. The music is an instrumental that reminds me of a tango in many ways, but is nothing spectacular nor is it really bad, it just 'is'. This is simply a bit of eye candy as the girls from Ladies v Butters pose naked for your enjoyment. Its not bad but then again its not good. So in conclusion: pictures of naked girls set to music in a 3 minute video. The end.
