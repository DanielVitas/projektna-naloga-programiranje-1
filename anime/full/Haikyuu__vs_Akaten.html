Synopsis

In Haikyuu!!: vs. Akaten, which takes place in the second season, Hinata and Kageyama fail a test and find that the make-up exam conflicts with their training camp.

Edit
Background

Haikyuu!! vs. Akaten (ハイキュー!! VS赤点) was screened at Jump Festa 2015 on November 3, 2015. It was later bundled with the 21st volume of the manga, released on May 2, 2016.
